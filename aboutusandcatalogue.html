About Us

We are a tech shop operating in Portland, Maine. We sell a lot of tech stuff, mainly robotics. We only do shipping in the New England area, so please consider coming by if you are outside our range. If you are looking for robots, please pass by our catalogue by either clicking the catalogue button on top or scroll down. If you are an enjoyer of robotics and wanted to sell your product here, please contact us using the contact button. We will answer your questions in our official Whatsapp business account.


We are a partner of multiple tech groups operating in various cities of New England. If you are a manager of one and want to partner with us, please contact us using the contact button. We will gladly answer your call.
